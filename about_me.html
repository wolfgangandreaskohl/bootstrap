/about_me

Born and raised in a remote rural area in Southern California the early part of my life was focused on creative growth and discovering a fascination with science and technology.

Once in college I discovered a continuing passion for technology and decided to pursue a degree in the management of information systems. Currently at San Diego State University using past experience, e.g. building computers and troubleshooting networks, and translating that knowledge into professional skills.

Other hobbies include producing music in Ableton Live and Fruityloops and web development. This website was developed using the Bootstrap front-end framework. Further information on Bootstrap may be found at their website: http://getbootstrap.com/. Tiled backgrounds are provided by: http://subtlepatterns.com/

If you would like to contact me to chat or discuss possible work opportunities feel free to send me an email, text message, or call using the information provided below.

Together, with science and technology, let's make this world a more creative and efficient place!
